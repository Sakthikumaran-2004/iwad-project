The Kijang Innova EV Concept is an EV conversion prototype based on the second-generation Kijang Innova that was first presented on 31 March 2022 in Indonesia.
The conversion was done by the Australian branch of SEA Electric, a company specialising in electric trucks conversion based in Melbourne, Australia.
Five prototypes were built; three in Melbourne and two in Indonesia.
The storage battery uses a 58.9 kWh lithium-ion unit with an estimated range of 280 km (175 miles).
The electric motor is rated at 134 kW (180 hp; 182 PS) and could generate up to 700 N⋅m (520 lb⋅ft) of torque.The claimed top speed is 120 km/h (75 mph).
